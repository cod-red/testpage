Gangtok Sightseeing

Discover the beauty and charm of Gangtok in a single day with these top destinations.

Ranka Monastery

Monastery is a Buddhist monastery near Ranka in Sikkim, North East India, about an hour's drive from Gangtok. It was completed in 1999.

Rumtek Monastery

The headquarters of Buddhism in India, this monastery is a significant cultural and spiritual site.

Ganesh Tok

Ganesh Tok is a small temple with viewpoint in Gangtok. Situated on top of a hill,the Kanchenjunga hill can be seen in its true form of glory from here and is at its best especially in the morning.

Enchey Monastery

The Enchey Monastery was established in 1909 above Gangtok, the capital city of Sikkim in the Northeastern Indian state. It belongs to the Nyingma order of Vajrayana Buddhism.

Tashi View Point

Provides exceptional views of the Kanchenjunga snow peaks, especially beautiful at sunrise and sunset.

Hanuman Tok

Hanuman Tok, dedicated to Lord Hanuman is situated near Ganesh Tok at an altitude of 7200 feet and offers an amazing view of the Kanchenjunga range.

Namgyal Institute of Tibetology

A museum and research institute focusing on Tibetan culture, established in 1957.

Gangtok Ropeway

Gangtok Ropeway offers an exhilarating cable car ride that treats visitors to stunning panoramic views of the city and the surrounding hills. Gliding above lush greenery, bustling streets, and charming neighborhoods

Ban Jhakri Falls Park

The park's statuary and other displays document the Ban Jhakri, or traditional shamanic healer who worships spirits living in caves around the falls

Adventure Activities in Sikkim

Experience the thrill of adventure in Sikkim with these exciting activities.

Paragliding Joyride

Soar high above the valleys and mountains, experiencing the thrill of flying with stunning panoramic views.

Camping

Reconnect with nature under the starry skies. Enjoy bonfires, music, and peaceful nights in the wild.

Trekking

Moderate day trek near Gangtok, Sikkim, that takes you through the Fambong Lho Wildlife Sanctuary to a viewpoint offering panoramic views of the Himalayas, including Mount Kanchenjunga.
