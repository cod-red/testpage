Tsango Lake, Nathula Pass & Zuluk

Explore Sikkims breathtaking gems in a memorable journey

Itinerary

1: Tsango Lake

Arrive in Gangtok and drive to Tsango Lake, a stunning glacial lake at 12,400 ft. Visit Baba Harbhajan Singh Mandir nearby.

2: Nathula Pass

Visit Nathula Pass, one of the highest motorable roads. Enjoy Himalayan views and learn about Indo-China history.

3: Zuluk

Drive through the Old Silk Route to Zuluk. Enjoy hairpin bends, sunrise points, and explore the village’s cultural heritage.
